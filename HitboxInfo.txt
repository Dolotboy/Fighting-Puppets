Hitbox:

Basic config:
	-Capsule Object
	-Disabled Mesh Renderer
	-Capsule Collider [OnTrigger]
	-Hitbox Script [PlayerObject is the GameObject with the health script]
	-Tag [Hitbox_...]

Weapon:
	Empty:
		-GameObject:
			-Collider [OnTrigger] {Disabled}
			-Weapon Script
			-Tag [Weapon]
		-Config visuals in Steam Inventory.../Prefabs/GamePlayer 
			WeaponHolder:[Player_TPose.../Hips/Spine/Spine1/Spine2/RightShoulder/RightArm/RightForeArm/RightHand]


Function: 
	When enabled weapon hits hitbox, sends the hitbox tag/damageModifier to Health.TakeHit
	TakeHit Checks the tag and adds the basic damage to damageModifier then calls takeDamage()
